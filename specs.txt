Interface: Form with an input field for the user's word. After submitting their word, the app outputs the scrabble score.

1.  Behavior: Convert user's word to all lower case letters.
    Input: 'I'
    Output: 'i'

2.  Behavior: Split user's word into component letters.
    Input: 'in'
    Output: 'i','n'

3.  Behavior: Assign numerical value to each letter in user's word.
    Input: 'i','n'
    Output: 1 , 1

4.  Behavior: Add values of letters together.
    Input: 1 , 1
    Output: 2
